Kha-224 Merul Badda
Dhaka 1212. Bangladesh
joined BRAC Business School as a Lecturer in September 2017. Prior to joining Brac University, he has completed a stint of 2.5 years as a Teaching Assistant at BRAC Business School. He also worked as a course instructor of general English proficiency development course conducted by BIL. He has done his BBA and MBA from BRAC Business School, Brac University. He managed to secure a CGPA of 4.00 in the MBA program.
While studying as an undergraduate student, he was actively involved in extracurricular activities. He was the president of Business and Economics Forum, BRAC University. He was one of the proud members of Bangladeshi Youth delegation to India-2014. He was also the honorary member of British Council Resource Center during 2013-2014.
At present, he is also serving as the assistant coordinator of the BBA Program at BRAC Business School.
