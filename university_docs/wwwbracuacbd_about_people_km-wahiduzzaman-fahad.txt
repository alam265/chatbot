K. M. Wahiduzzaman Fahad
joined BRAC Business School (BBS) as a Lecturer in Business Analytics in January 2026. He brings an industry background to academia, having previously worked as a Data Systems Analyst in his last role at EquipmentShare, a U.S.-based company. He is an alumnus of BRAC University, where he earned his Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) degree, and later completed a Master of Science in Business Analytics from Texas A&amp;M University–Commerce. With experience in data analysis, reporting, and data engineering, he envisions introducing hands-on, experiential learning in the classroom.
