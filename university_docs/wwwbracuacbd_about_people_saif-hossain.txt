is an Associate Professor at BRAC Business School, BRAC University, where he joined in 2010. Currently, he is also serving as the Director of the BBA Program, a role he has held since May 2019. In 2021, he played a key role in introducing a revised curriculum for the BBA program. As the program director, he has significantly revamped various processes to better serve over 2,000 students. During his tenure, he has raised over a million BDT in funding, organized various workshops, seminars and training programs. During the pandemic, he was instrumental in training faculty to transition to BuX, the online teaching platform, which is powered by edX technology used by leading institutions like Harvard and MIT. He also successfully facilitated the formation of the first-ever BBS Alumni Advisory Board, comprising notable alumni from different industries.
Saif is also a PhD candidate in Accounting at Putra Business School. With over a decade of teaching experience, he has expertise in Financial Accounting, Management Accounting, Business Communication, and various other business-related subjects at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels. His research focuses on accounting literacy and financial behavior, particularly among micro and small entrepreneurs.
In addition to his academic career, Saif has entrepreneurial experience, having co-founded and managed a digitized center for secondary education in Dhaka. The venture, unfortunately, had to be liquidated during the COVID-19 pandemic. However, this experience enriched his understanding of the challenges faced by entrepreneurs, especially in volatile environments. Passionate about fostering entrepreneurship, Saif promotes entrepreneurial initiatives among students. He has organized a dedicated platform for students and graduates with their own startups and ventures, coining the term "BRACUPRENEURS" to represent this vibrant community of emerging entrepreneurs of BRAC University.
Throughout his career, Saif has held numerous administrative and advisory roles, contributing to the development and success of BRAC University. He has supervised over 150 undergraduate interns and 40 postgraduate interns, demonstrating a commitment to mentoring the next generation of business professionals. Recognized for his dedication to education, Saif received the Inaugural Service to the University Award in 2023 and was nominated for Best Teacher in 2014. His passion for education, research, and entrepreneurship is evident in his contributions both inside and outside the classroom.
