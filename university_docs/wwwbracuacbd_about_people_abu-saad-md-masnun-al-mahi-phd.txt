Dr. Abu Saad Md. Masnun Al Mahi
is currently serving as an Associate Professor (Finance) at the BRAC Business School, BRAC University. His academic career spans multiple countries and institutions, including previous roles at the University of Liberal Arts Bangladesh and as an Adjunct Lecturer at Sultan Sharif Ali Islamic University, Brunei.
He earned his PhD in Finance from the University of Malaya with the “University of Malaya Financial Aid” and his MSc (Research) in Finance from Universiti Putra Malaysia under the prestigious “Khazanah Foundation Scholarship” (by the Malaysian Government). Before entering academia, Dr. Mahi began his career as a Management Trainee Officer at Mercantile Bank Ltd., Bangladesh, and later accumulated more than five years of combined research and professional experience in banking, research, and data analytics across Bangladesh and Malaysia. This included contributing to projects funded by the Malaysian Ministry of Higher Education and the University of Malaya, as well as serving as an honorary fellow at a research, data analytics, and training firm in Malaysia.
Dr. Mahi’s research focuses on sustainability from a finance perspective, with emphasis on energy, climate, and community. His work is published in leading journals by Elsevier, Emerald, Sage, and Springer, indexed in WoS, Scopus, ABS, and ABDC, including the Journal of Environmental Management, Journal of International Financial Markets, Institutions & Money, Finance Research Letters, Journal of Cleaner Production, Research in International Business and Finance, among others. He has received multiple competitive research grants, actively collaborates with international scholars, and regularly presents at international conferences. In addition, he serves as a reviewer for several high-impact journals in his areas of expertise, including Journal of Business Ethics, Journal of Cleaner Production, Resources Policy, International Journal of Finance & Economics, Financial Innovation, etc.
