joined BRAC Business School as “Associate Professor” in January 2014. Prior to joining BRAC University, Riyashad taught seven years as a full-time Senior lecturer at School of Business, North South University, Bangladesh. He has also extensive academic and professional working experiences in Canada and UK. He was a recipient of the prestigious Dean’s Honours List for consecutive two years at St. Francis Xavier University, Canada.
