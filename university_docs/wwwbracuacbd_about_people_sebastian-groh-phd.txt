Passionate to drive the 5D's fuelling our energy future: Decentralization, Decarbonization, Disruption, Democratization & Digitization! In line with these believes, together with his colleagues at SOLshare, Sebastian installed the world's first cyber-physical peer-to-peer solar sharing grid in a remote area of Bangladesh as the founder and managing director of SOLshare, a start-up based out of Bangladesh that has set out to develop the future of energy.
Dr. Groh is a 2013 Stanford Ignite Fellow from Stanford Graduate School of Business and holds a PhD from Aalborg University and the Postgraduate School Microenergy Systems at the TU Berlin where he wrote his doctoral thesis on the role of energy in development processes, energy poverty & technical innovations, with a special focus on Bangladesh.
