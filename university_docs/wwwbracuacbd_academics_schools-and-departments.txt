Schools and Departments
BRAC Business School
The BRAC Business School (BBS) offers an undergraduate program: Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) and two graduate programs: Master of Business Administration (MBA) and Executive Master of Business Administration (EMBA).
School of Data and Sciences
The School of Data & Sciences is composed of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and the Mathematics & Natural Sciences Department (MNS).
BSRM School of Engineering
The BSRM School of Engineering currently hosts the Department of Electrical & Electronic Engineering (EEE).
School of Law
The School of Law at BRAC University is a gateway through which students are prepared for careers in law, in administrative services, the judiciary and in the development sector.

School of Humanities and Social Sciences
Founded in 2019, the school supervises the university's teaching and research activities in the liberal arts.
School of General Education
The general education program provides a common education foundation to all students before they take courses specific to their major and minor fields of study.
