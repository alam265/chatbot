Dr. Nusrat Hafiz
an Assistant Professor of Entrepreneurship and International Business, also holds the position of Director of the Women Entrepreneurship & Empowerment Cell at BRAC Business School. In this dual role, she is passionately committed to empowering and championing the next generation of leaders. Armed with a Ph.D. from Putra Business School (AACSB Accredited), her research contribution has garnered her "quality journal publication awards" in multiple categories, recognizing her as one of the top contributors to Q1 journals. She possesses a decade's worth of experience in academia and half a decade of corporate exposure, complemented by her deep-rooted family values and unwavering work ethics. Dr. Hafiz's teaching philosophy prioritizes pragmatic approaches, while her research interests span strategic management, organizational sustainability, women empowerment, digitalization and innovation, dynamic capabilities and social entrepreneurship. Her personal slogan, "Empowering Students, Every Step of the Way," embodies her commitment to student-centricity.
