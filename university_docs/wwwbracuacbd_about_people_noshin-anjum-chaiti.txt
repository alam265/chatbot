Ms. Noshin Anjum Chaiti
joined Brac Business School (BBS), Brac University as a full-time lecturer in September,2023. Previously she worked at Primeasia University as a full-time lecturer. Other than having four years of experience in teaching, she also has experience in arranging numerous events such as workshops, webinars, seminars, and cultural functions.
She completed her undergraduate degree from North South University, and she pursued her MSc. in Investment Fund Management from University of Glasgow, UK with scholarship. She achieved the academic excellence award for her MSc. Degree. As a student she actively participated in competitions and cultural functions.
Her research interests include capital markets, securities, and corporate finance.
