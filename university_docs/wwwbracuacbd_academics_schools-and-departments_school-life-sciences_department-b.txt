Welcome from Department Chairperson
Academic Focus and Research Culture
The Department fosters active learning and research that link science with real-world challenges and the SDGs. We have seven dedicated research labs where all the faculty members are doing active research in different fields like Molecular Biotechnology, Environmental Biotechnology, Plant Biotechnology, Biosafety, Radiation and Nano Biotechnology, Microbial Genetics, Genomics, Computational Biology etc. Department sought for research funding opportunities from different Government and nongovernment organizations and secure funding from many sources like HEAT (Higher Education Acceleration and Transformation) from UGC, MoST (Ministry of Science and Technology), RSGI (Research Seed Grant Initiative) from Brac University, BAS USDA PALS Endowment fund etc. Department have active research collaboration with many public and private research institutes and Universities of home and abroad.
