Dr. M. Nazmul Islam
is an Associate Professor of BRAC Business School (BBS) at the BRAC University, Bangladesh. Dr. Islam completed his Ph.D. in Human Resource Management from the University of Malaya (UM), Malaysia. Prior to joining BBS, Dr. Islam served as a full-time faculty member at the business school of two different universities named University of Liberal Arts Bangladesh and University of Asia Pacific as an Assistant Professor where he taught human resource management, strategic human resource management, managerial negotiation, strategic management, and principles of management courses in undergraduate and graduate program. Before starting his Ph.D., he served as a Research & Development Officer and Manager, BBA Program at the School of Business at Independent University, Bangladesh (IUB). Dr. Islam completed his MBA and BBA from the School of Business, IUB. Dr. Islam has published several articles in different indexed journals. He is currently serving as an editorial board member at Frontiers in Psychology (Organizational Psychology) journal. Dr. Islam is an active reviewer in many reputed journals published by renowned publishers such as Elsevier, Emerald, Sage, Springer, and Wiley. His research interest includes HRM, leadership, and organizational change management.
