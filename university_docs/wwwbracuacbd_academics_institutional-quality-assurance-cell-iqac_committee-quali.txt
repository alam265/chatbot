University Quality Assurance Committee (UQAC)
Overseeing matters of Quality Assurance and the operation of the Institutional Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)
Composition of the UQAC:
Vice-Chancellor (Chair of the UQAC)
Pro Vice-Chancellor
All Deans/Directors of Schools/Institutes
Director of IQAC
UGC representative
Finance representative (when required)
Associate Director of IQAC (Member Secretary of UQAC)
Two other senior academics (with expertise in IT) nominated by the Syndicate, on the advice of the Vice-Chancellor
The University Quality Assurance Committee (UQAC) will report to Academic Council
School Quality Assurance Committee (SQAC)
Implement the decisions taken by the University Quality Assurance Committee (UQAC) on key issues of quality assurance at School and/or Department
The composition of SQAC:
Dean/Director of the school/institute (Chair)
Associate/Assistant Deans of the school (if any)
All heads/chairs from departments within the school
Two other members of Faculty appointed by the Dean/Director
Secretary: nominated by the Chair
The Committee will report to the University Quality Assurance Committee (UQAC)
Program Self-Assessment Committee (PSAC)
Program Self-Assessment Committee (PSAC) in each Department/School/Institute are responsible for
Performing periodic self-assessment of the program(s)
Monitoring activities taken under Process Management (PM) and Continuous Quality Improvement (CQI) of the program
The composition of the PSAC will be as follows:
Chairman/Director of the Department/Institute or a senior faculty of the Department/Institute (Chair of PSAC)
Minimum Two other faculty members from the program or group of programs (Members)
