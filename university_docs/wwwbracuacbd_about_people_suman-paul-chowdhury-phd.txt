Suman Paul Chowdhury
, Associate Professor of Accounting at BRAC Business School, has been a faculty member at BRAC University since 2006. He has over a decade of experience in teaching, research, and academic leadership in the field of accounting. In 2015, he earned his doctoral degree from The University of Western Australia (UWA), having been awarded the prestigious Endeavour Postgraduate Award in 2011 by the Australian Government. His research interests span a wide range of topics, including principal-agent relationships, corporate governance, executive compensation, corporate reporting, family-owned firms, knowledge sharing behavior, financial literacy, and earnings management. He also serves as a reviewer for several esteemed academic journals.
