Dr. Abu Zakir Chowdhury
joined BRAC University as an Associate Professor of Finance in January 2025. Previously, he worked as a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Turku in Finland. Dr. Chowdhury’s research interest is corporate finance. He has published articles on insider trading and initial public offerings in esteemed journals such as the British Accounting Review and Global Finance Journal. Currently, he is working on equity issuance, corporate failure, and ESG.
