Dr.Tarnima Warda Andalib
is currently working as an Assistant Professor, BRAC Business School at BRAC University, Bangladesh. Earlier she was employed as a Post-Doctoral Fellow and Lecturer (Tutorial Laboratory of Strategic Management through MonsoonSim Game Simulation Course) in the School of Management at University Sains Malaysia, the only APEX (Accelerated Programs for Excellence) University in Malaysia from 2019 to 2021.
Dr. Tarnima earned her Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Technology Management with Doctoral Scheme Scholarship (DSS) and PGRS grant from the Faculty of Industrial Management at University Malaysia Pahang in Malaysia focusing on the Organization behaviour, Human Resource Management and Assimilated Technology Framework development in 2018. Simultaneously, she has worked as a Lecturer of Strategic Management, Management Information System and Risk Management Courses as well as the Project Manager of e-learning OTM in the Faculty of Industrial Management at University Malaysia Pahang from 2012 till 2018. During this time she has earned few academic excellence awards like earning the 6th position award in the entire competition among all the Wings and the 4th position in the Technology Innovation Wing in the 3 mins competitions in the consecutive years 2013 and 2015. She also received Silver medals consecutively in two different CITREX Research Competitions in the years 2014 and 2015.
Previously, Dr.Tarnima earned 3.89 CGPA (97% marks) in her Master’s in Business Administration(MBA) at University of Development Alternative upon completion with 50% scholarship, where her major was Human Resource Management. Prior to that she has earned her Bachelor’s in Computer Science (CSC) with a major in Software Programming and a minor in Management Information System(MIS) from North South University with 82% marks, prior to which she also received Joynul Memorial Scholarship (1st Grade 2nd, Silver Medal in Science Group) in her school life. She also has received 7th position in the Intra-NSU Programming Contest in 2004 by using C++.
Additionally, Dr. Tarnima also has Corporate skills and Experiences for 8 years, where she has worked in few corporate companies. In the beginning of her corporate career she worked in the CS Laboratory at North South University as a Lab Assistant for almost a year, then she continued to work as an Intern (Dynamic web developer) in the IT department of AB Bank Ltd, then she worked as a product management executive and web developer in the Product management team of dataedge Ltd for few months and afterwards also worked as a database engineer and research analyst in PKSF-InM (Institute of Microfinance). Nevertheless, she spent most of her corporate life in Grameenphone Ltd, one of the group companies under Norwegian Telenor Group, as an Officer in IT division and as a Senior Executive in the Technology Division consecutively, where she has worked for almost 5 and half years. During her corporate era, she has worked in various units and projects as a Project Manager as well as a Team member in IT Process Re-engineering, LTStrategy, GP IT Architecture, Assurance-Security and Compliance, MIS reporting and decision making dashboard on ICX, VAS Subcentre, BSc-MSS Transmission, IVR Hits-Stroke etc, Multi-Layer sales Application design, E1 reconciliation by getting into TNMS systems through lease-sublease, dynamic web development projects using PhP, Javascript, Java and etc. She is an ITIL®V3 (EXIN Group, Singapore) Certified Professional receiving 90-94% marks in the Certification Exam, besides being a DMAIC via SigmaXL Certified Professional also.
Dr.Tarnima’s expertise is in the Qualitative Research Approach specifically in the Grounded theory with MCS (Multiple case studies), Epistemological, Ontological and Methodical Paradigms-theories where she has used NVIVOMac, AMOS most of the times but also has experiences to work in the Smart-PLS, ATLAS.ti while applying some Mix-method research techniques. Being a novice researcher, Dr. Tarnima has more than 30 Academic Publications like journal articles, books, book chapter/s and conference proceedings indexed in Scopus, Wos and others, some of which are in the databases of Inderscience, MDPI, KnE Social Sciences, IOP Series, Elsevier and etc. receiving so far 111 citations, h-index:7 and i-index:5.
Dr. Tarnima’s current interest areas are Block-chain Technology, Innovation Management, Entrepreneurial Bricolage, Organizational Behaviour and Organizational Psychology, Assimilated Technology Framework, Human Resource Management Development, Integration of Employee Rights and Grievance Management system. Besides, her academic works, she has a strong knack towards Literature and as a result she has seven published poetry books, three of which are in English and the rest four are in her mother-tongue Bangla along with some other non-academic write-ups, some of which can be found in rokomari.com or in amazon.com and in other platforms etc. During her school life she also achieved “Bishya Shahityo Award” as a prominent library-reader. She has been an active photographer at TTL (Through the Lens) for a brief time during that time she has achieved “Green award” for her photography named “Nature’s Revulsion” and “Peaceful Zephyr”. Some of her other photographs got exhibited in some Photography exhibitions in the country as well.
