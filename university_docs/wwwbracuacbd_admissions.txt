BRAC University always pays close attention to convenient approach in terms of Admission Procedures. The application process is online and applicants can apply from the convenience of their home. The entire process of admission goes through an intensely selective method where applicants are asked to follow each phase precisely within the given framework of instructions. To get admitted into BRAC University, applicants must qualify in the admission test (written and an interview) after submitting their online application form.
As the admission test is a highly competitive one, applicants are required to perform satisfactorily in the examination in order to secure their seats.
