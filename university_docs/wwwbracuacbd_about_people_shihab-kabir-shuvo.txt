After completing his HSC from cumilla cadet college he was selected as a recipient of “international student excellency award” from Eastern Illinois University, USA, which Is an AACSB accredited University. He completed his bachelor’s in management information systems there and immediately after graduation he started his MBA at the same school in strategic management concentration with full ride scholarship. He has worked in various prestigious positions on the campus and outside during his 7 years of stay in the United states.
Before joining BRAC University he worked at Eastern Illinois University’s library, student leadership office, study abroad office and in projects lead by faculties.
He would like to utilize his learning of his education and abroad life in BRAC University and continue learning to ensure the best learning experience for the students here.
