Dr. Md. Kausar Alam
is working as an Associate Professor at BRAC Business School (BBS), BRAC University. He started his career at BRACU as an Assistant Professor in September 2021. Before joining BRACU, he served as a senior lecturer at the University of South Asia, Dhaka. He completed his PhD from Universiti Putra Malaysia (UPM). He graduated on time from UPM. He completed his MBA & BBA from the Department of Accounting and Information Systems, Jagannath University Dhaka. Dr. Kausar was actively involved in developing OBE-based accounting course curriculum at BRAC Business School. He received research grants from BBS in 2023 and 2024.
Dr. Kausar is currently working on several qualitative and quantitative research projects. He published numerous articles, one book and three book chapters in several international Scopus and Web of Science indexed journals and publishers respectively in the area of accounting, finance, Islamic finance, corporate governance, and qualitative research. His research works have been published in Journal of Applied Accounting Research, International Review of Financial Analysis, Applied Economics, Asian Review of Accounting, Pacific Accounting Review, Journal of Islamic Accounting and Business Research, Heliyon, Journal of Economics Finance and Administrative Science, Qualitative Research in Organizations and Management, Asian Journal of Accounting Research, Journal of Public Affairs, International Journal of Ethics and Systems, Journal of Asian Finance, Economics and Business, International Journal of Sociology and Social Policy and Asian Economic and Financial Review.
He serves as an ad-hoc reviewer of different accounting and finance journals. He participated in international seminars and research workshops. He has expertise in qualitative research and NVivo software analysis. He is a trainer and facilitator in qualitative research and NVivo software. He is involved with social activities and voluntary functions.
