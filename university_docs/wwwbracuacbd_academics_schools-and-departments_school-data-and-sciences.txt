School of Data and Sciences
The School of Data & Sciences is composed of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and the Department of Mathematics & Natural Sciences (MNS). The school's focus is core computer science and engineering and fundamental sciences. Using the combined expertise of both the CSE and MNS departments it aims to be a leader in data science and related areas.
