is a Senior Lecturer at BRAC Business School, BRAC University. Rifat brings with her years of multifaceted experience in various management and academic roles in Oceania, South Asia, and North America. Rifat has impeccable communication, relationship management, people skills and a solution-driven mindset that stands alone.
Prior to joining BRAC University, Rifat worked as a Management Consultant at Ernst & Young (EY) Canada and KPMG Canada — two of the world’s foremost consulting firms where she supported businesses in their transformation journey towards achieving greater performance. Rifat nurtures a strong interest towards HR planning and transformation, developing talent strategies and managing through change that might be ambiguous at times but extremely exciting and challenging in nature requiring to be dealt with utmost sincerity, integrity, and honesty. Rifat has also worked at Chevron Bangladesh, a U.S. based Fortune 500 energy giant for over three years supporting their HR Operations in areas such as HRIS, Recruitment, and Learning and Development with demonstrating skills in various HRIS Platforms.
Earlier in her career,
held faculty positions at BRAC University and North South University, where she taught undergraduate courses and pursued research focused on organizational effectiveness, employee job satisfaction, and organizational justice. Her scholarly contributions have been published in multiple peer-reviewed international journals, reflecting her ongoing commitment to advancing knowledge in human resource management and organizational development.
Rifat believes through hard work, dedication and a little dream in your heart you can always open new possibilities in life.
