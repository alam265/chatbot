A Prime Location in Dhaka City
Located at Kha 224 Pragati Sarani, Merul Badda , Dhaka 1212, Bangladesh, the new BRAC University campus is easily accessible from key areas like Hatirjhil, Rampura, Aftabnagar, Banasree, Niketon, Mohakhali, Gulshan, Banani, and Baridhara. Spanning over 5 acres with a building area of 1,700,000 square feet, this campus is a central hub for education in the heart of Dhaka.
Innovative Architecture for a Sustainable Future
Designed by WOHA Designs Pte Ltd., a renowned Singaporean architectural firm, the 13-story campus includes three basements and has the capacity to accommodate 12,000-15,000 students. Around half the land is dedicated to the structure, with the remainder focused on promoting biodiversity through a lake, green spaces, and plantations.
Draped in Green
Both interior and exterior greenery provide shade, reduce temperature, and enhance air quality.
The Cafeteria
The cafeteria is a welcoming space for socializing and enjoying a meal.
Salient Feature
The Campus is a 13-story building with three basements, designed to accommodate around 12,000-15,000 students. It's an environmentally conscious structure, allowing natural light and air to flow freely. Nearly half of the land is dedicated to the building itself, while the remainder is utilized for a water body, lake, plantation, and other features aimed at promoting biodiversity.
Sun-drenched Campus
The design of the building largely utilizes natural sunlight, thereby reducing the need for artificial lighting.
Nourished with Rainwater
Harvested rainwater and a cleansing biotope meet 20% of the campus's water demand, especially for waterbodies, vegetation, toilet flushing, and firefighting.
Powered by Renewable Energy
A quarter of the campus's energy demand is met by solar panels on the roof, highlighting the commitment to sustainability.
Rooftop Green Lawn
The greenery, sand, and gravel on the rooftop have therapeutic effects, promoting wellness.
Jogging Track
The rooftop jogging track is a great place to get some exercise while enjoying a beautiful view.
