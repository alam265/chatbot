Dr. Mohammad Enamul Hoque
is working as an associate professor at BRAC Business School, BRAC University. He started his career at BRACU as an assistant professor in January 2022. Dr. Hoque formerly worked at a well-known asset management firm that specialised in government-tradable securities. He was in charge of the research and trading teams. Dr. Hoque is actively involved in developing OBE-based finance course curricula at the BRAC Business School. He also awarded several research grants from BRAC University, Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia, and Universiti Malaya.
His research interests are in the areas of applied economics and finance, asset pricing, energy finance, energy economics, monetary economics, and financial consumer behavior.  Dr. Hoque's works appeared in  high quality and top ranked journals,  as such Applied Economics, Borsa Istanbul Review, Climatic Change, Economic Analysis and Policy, Economic Research, Energy Economics, Environment, Development and Sustainability, Global Finance Journal, International Journal of Emerging Markets, International Journal of Finance and Economics, International Review of Financial Analysis, International Review of Economics and Finance, Journal of Cleaner Production, Pacific-Basin Finance Journal, Resources Policy, Renewable Energy, The Journal of Futures Markets, and Service Industries Journal.
