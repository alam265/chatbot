Ms. Takmilla Tabassum
is currently working as a Lecturer at BRAC Business School. Previously, she worked as a full-time lecturer at Primeasia University and an adjunct lecturer at BRAC University. She completed an MSc in International Strategic Marketing with merit from the University of Glasgow and a Bachelor of Business Administration from North South University (graduating cum laude). Her research interests include prosocial behavior, omnichannel management, sustainability, strategic management, consumption ethics, consumer behavior, innovation ecosystems, and brand management.
