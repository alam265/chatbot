Tausif Bari joined BRAC University as the Manager of Advancement in 2020, as an Adjunct Lecturer in 2021, and as a full-time Senior Lecturer in 2022. His past working experiences include University of Sydney (Teaching Assistant), LightCastle Partners (Business Consultant) and UNIQLO Bangladesh (Production Coordinator).
Mr. Bari completed Master of Professional Accounting with distinction from the University of Sydney, and Bachelor of Business Administration from North South University (graduating summa cum laude). He has been inducted into the Beta Gamma Sigma society and has been awarded the CPA Australia Prize No. 1, the Business Leaders Postgraduate Scholarship and the Vice Chancellor’s International Scholarship at the University of Sydney.
