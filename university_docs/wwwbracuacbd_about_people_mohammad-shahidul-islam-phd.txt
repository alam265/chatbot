Dr. Mohammad Shahidul Islam
is an Associate Professor at BRAC Business School, BRAC University, whose academic journey reflects a commitment to advancing knowledge in marketing and hospitality. His research spans diverse areas, including ethics, experience co-creation, service innovation, and interpersonal relations. Dr. Islam's work, published in prestigious Scopus Q1 and elite ABDC A* journals, has significantly shaped contemporary discussions in these fields. He is the proud recipient of the CAUTHE Best Paper Award (Australia) 2023, underscoring the importance and relevance of his contributions. Beyond his research, Dr. Islam is an ad hoc reviewer for leading academic journals in business and hospitality, published by SAGE, Elsevier, Emerald, Wiley, and Taylor & Francis. This supports the development of high-caliber scholarly work. He teaches courses such as Principles of Marketing, Service Marketing, Strategic Brand Management, Strategic Marketing, Marketing Management, and Consumer Behavior as an educator. He inspires students to foster analytical thinking and acquire practical skills tailored to industry needs through these. Dr. Islam extends his expertise to the public through over 1,000 op-eds and literary pieces in national and international outlets, sharing insights on ethical marketing, sustainable tourism, and service innovation. Dr. Islam's academic influence is global; he has presented his research and engaged in scholarly dialogues in countries including Hong Kong, England, Scotland, Bhutan, Thailand, Turkey, Australia, and the USA. These international experiences have enriched his perspectives and fostered collaborations with diverse intellectual communities, amplifying his impact in marketing, hospitality, and tourism studies. Dr. Islam's research continues to break new ground, exploring the intricacies of marketing, hospitality experiences, visual research, phenomenology, and service innovation. His work reflects a unique blend of philosophical inquiry and practical insight, driving academic discussions while delivering tangible solutions to real-world challenges.
