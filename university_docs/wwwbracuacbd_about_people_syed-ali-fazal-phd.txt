Dr. Syed Ali Fazal
is currently serving as an Associate Professor at BRAC Business School, BRAC University. Before joining the BRAC family, he served as an Associate Professor at the Department of Business Administration, Daffodil International University Bangladesh; as an Associate Professor at the Faculty of Business Administration, University of Science and Technology Chittagong, as Assistant Professor at the College of Business Administration, International University of Business Agriculture and Technology, Bangladesh; and as lecturer at the School of Business, University of Information Technology and Sciences, Bangladesh. Apart from teaching, Dr. Fazal held several administrative positions, including Dean of Faculty and Director of IQAC at the University of Science and Technology Chittagong; as well as Coordinator for the MBA program at the International University of Business Agriculture and Technology.
Dr. Fazal earned his PhD (Management) from the Faculty of Entrepreneurship and Business, University Malaysia Kelantan (No. 1 Entrepreneurial University of Malaysia). His Postdoctoral experience is from UKM-Graduate School of Business (AACSB Accredited), Universiti Kembangan Malaysia (QS World Ranking-138). He authored more than 50 Academic Journal Articles, a few Conference papers, and a Research Book and managed to secure national and international academic grants as well. His publications are cited more than 2300 times with an H Index 22 (Google Scholar). His research interests revolve around Quantitative Social Science, Knowledge Transfer and Business Management, particularly focusing on Organizational Sustainability and Sustainable Development. Dr. Fazal is a founding member and lifetime fellow of Bangladesh Society for Private University Academics (BSPUA); Honorary Faculty Member at Novel Global Community Educational Foundation (NGCEF), Australia; Associate Academic Editor for Plos One, Editorial Board Member for PEOPLE: International Journal of Social Sciences; Member of International Society for Research and Development (ISRD); and Reviewer for several Academic Journals.
