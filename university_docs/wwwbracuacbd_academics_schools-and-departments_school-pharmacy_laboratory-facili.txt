The Department of Pharmacy at BRAC University has SEVEN
state-of-the-art laboratories
for world class teaching and research activities. They are:
1. Pharmaceutical Technology and Biopharmaceutics Lab
2. Inorganic and Phytochemistry Lab
3. Organic Pharmacy and Medicinal Chemistry Lab
4. Pharmaceutical Microbiology, Physiology and Pharmacology Lab
5. Advanced Analytical Lab and Instrument Room (under construction)
6. Pharmaceutical Analysis and Physical Pharmacy Lab, and
7. Computer Lab.
