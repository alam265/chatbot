Undergraduate Programs
BRAC University offers a broad and diverse selection of undergraduate programs, designed to provide students with the flexibility to align their academic pursuits with personal ambitions and career goals. Our programs emphasize a broad-based education, fostering critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills across various disciplines. Whether one is interested in the arts, sciences, business, or technology, our undergraduate courses ensure a well-rounded education that equips you with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.
Postgraduate Programs
Our comprehensive range of postgraduate programs at BRAC University are tailored to equip students with advanced academic expertise and specialized skills. These programs foster intellectual growth and professional excellence, helping students excel in their chosen fields and making meaningful contributions to industry, academia, and society.
Postgraduate Diploma
BRAC University’s Postgraduate Diploma programs are specifically designed to offer professionals, practical, hands-on knowledge and advanced skills in niche areas. Unlike full-length postgraduate degrees, these diploma programs are shorter in duration, making them ideal for those looking to quickly deepen their expertise or transition into new industries. They ensure a competitive edge in today's dynamic job market by focusing on applied learning in specialized fields.
Certificate Courses
Our certificate courses, offered through various schools and institutes, deliver focused, targeted training in specialized fields. These courses, typically ranging from a few weeks to several months in duration, are ideal for professionals seeking to sharpen their skills or explore new areas of interest. With a blend of theoretical insights and practical applications, certificate programs provide an excellent opportunity for skill enhancement in a time-efficient manner.
Short Courses
BRAC University’s short courses offer a fast-track to skill acquisition in specific disciplines. Typically lasting from one day to several days, these courses are tailored for professionals or individuals with limited time, providing concentrated learning experiences. They help participants quickly gain new expertise, improve current skills, or explore new professional interests in a brief, focused timeframe.
Visiting Programs
Our visiting programs welcome both local and international students as well as professionals to engage in specialized courses and research opportunities. These programs allow participants to collaborate with BRAC University’s renowned faculty, experience the vibrant campus life, and immerse themselves in a unique academic environment, all while gaining valuable cross-national exposure.

Exchange Programs
BRAC University’s exchange programs provide students with the unique opportunity to study abroad, offering immersive experiences that foster both academic growth and cultural exchange. These programs allow students to engage with diverse educational systems, collaborate with international peers, and broaden their global perspectives. By participating in an exchange, students not only enhance their academic and professional skills but also develop cross-cultural understanding, adaptability, and a global outlook—qualities highly valued in today’s interconnected world.
BRAC University-SOAS University of London Joint PhD Program
The joint PhD programme between SOAS University of London and BRAC University in Bangladesh offers doctoral training in the fields of development studies, development economics, the politics of development, and related fields, resulting in a PhD in the Political Economy of Development.
The joint PhD programme harnesses the benefits of the world class academic programme offered by the SOAS Doctoral School with the exceptional opportunity for testing theories of development in the field offered by BRAC University and its highly-regarded research institute, the BRAC Institute of Governance and Development.
