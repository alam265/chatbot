Policies & Procedures
BRAC University follows a model of higher education consisting of semesters, courses, credit hours, continuous evaluation and letter grading.
Institutional Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)
Quality assurance in tertiary education is a global practice now. The Institutional Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) of Brac University was established in July 2015 in order to ensure quality education at the tertiary level.
Open Society University Network (OSUN)
BRAC University is the first university from South Asia to be part of the Open Society University Network (OSUN).
