Mr. Ahsan graduated
from Queen’s University, Canada with a Master’s degree in Human Resource Management and Industrial Relations. Before that Mr. Ahsan was a graduate of North South University’s School of Business where he graduated with a Bachelors degree in Business Administration (majoring in Marketing and Finance). Currently, he is teaching as a lecturer at BRAC Business School at BRAC University. His research interests include work-life balance issues in organizations, employee welfare and organizational psychology in industrial settings.
