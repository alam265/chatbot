BRAC University is dedicated to becoming a leading institution of the Global South, redefining academic excellence to foster societal impact and human flourishing. With a liberal arts approach, BRAC University promotes innovative thinking, critical analysis, and the skills needed to address contemporary challenges.
As a leading private university in Bangladesh, BRAC University is accredited by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and approved by the Ministry of Education. Rooted in BRAC's legacy, the university instills a commitment to national development and prepares students to thrive in a knowledge-based economy.
We foster an inclusive and dynamic environment that values excellence, diversity, and creativity, offering career opportunities across academic, administrative, and technical fields. Whether contributing to cutting-edge research, enhancing student experiences, or supporting operations, your work will shape future changemakers.
As part of the BRAC family—the world’s largest NGO—BRAC University is committed to transformative education, impactful research, and advancing human values for sustainable development. Join us in shaping the future of education and driving meaningful change.
