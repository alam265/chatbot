Md (Mohammad) Abdul Hoque
had been awarded the degrees of Ph. D. (1994) and M. Sc. (Res.) (1991) in Management Science from Lancaster University Management School, England VIA Common Wealth scholarship. He obtained M. Sc. in Pure Mathematics (
) in 1979 and B. Sc. Honors in Mathematics (
) in 1978 from Dhaka University, Bangladesh. He had been trained professionally in Programming languages (C++, PASCAL, BASIC, COBOL) and System Analyses and Design.
Md Abdul Hoque joined Brac Business School of Brac University as Professor of Supply Chain Management on September 2019. He worked as Professor in Supply Chain Management at North South University (2016 – 2019). He worked as Associate Professor (2008 – 2016) and Senior Lecturer (2004 – 2008) at the Department of Mathematical and Computing Sciences of Universiti Brunei Darussalam. Also, Dr Hoque worked as Senior Lecturer (2002 – 2004) at the School of Mathematical Sciences, Universiti Sains Malaysia. Moreover, he worked at the Department of Mathematics (Lecturer – Professor) of Jahangirnagar University (1987 – 2002) Dhaka Bangladesh. Before that he worked at Dhaka City College. Besides, he had been an adjunct faculty at North South University (2001 -2002) and International University of Business Agriculture and Technology (1995 – 2000).  In addition, he taught as a tutor at Lancaster University for one year.
He had responsibly undertaken academic management roles as Chairman of the Department of Management of North South University (January’16 – January’18) and the Department of Mathematical and Computing Sciences of Universiti Brunei Darussalam (January’13 – December 15). Besides, he worked as students’ attachment coordinator, academic council members, syllabus committee members, admission committee members, examination committee members, external examiners, etc. both at home and abroad.
His experience in supervision of research students includes completion of three Ph. D. students and three M. Sc. (res.) students. His research papers have appeared in European Journal of Operational Research, International Journal of Production Economics, International Journal of Production Research, Computers & Operations Research, Journal of the Operations Research Society, Applied Soft Computing, International Journal of System Sciences, Opsearch etc. He has been serving on editorial board of international journals and reviewing of research papers of reputed international journals. He presented research papers in national and international conferences and seminars as an invited speaker, keynote speaker and general presenter. He attained six research project grants and completed them successfully as principal investigator. He has been a member of graduate research & scholarship & faculty research committees at different universities both at home and abroad.
