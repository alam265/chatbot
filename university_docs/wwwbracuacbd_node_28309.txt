BUReD publishes journal on research themes
Publish Date:
November 4th, 2024
BRAC University Research for Development Club (BUReD) has published a journal featuring themes of some of the research undertaken by students and faculty members of BRAC University this year.
“REFLECTION, Volume - I (June 03, 2024)” is the first publication of the BUReD compiled with support from Ayesha Abed Library and the Office of Co-curricular Activities (OCA).
The topics include wastewater management, Palestinians’ future, understanding procrastination, workplace safety, organizational culture, future of banking, machine and deep learning, artificial intelligence, nanotechnology in drug delivery, the pandemic, metro rail, advancements in prosthetics and cryptocurrency.
The publication ceremony was held at a lecture theatre on BRAC University’s Merul Badda campus on 30 October 2024.
The guests included Registrar Dr. David Dowland, Librarian Hasina Afroz, Dr. Tarnima Warda Andalib, BUReD Adviser and Assistant Professor of BRAC Business School, Md. Saif Kabir, BUReD Co-Advisor and Lecturer of the Office of Academic Advising, and Syed Shahnawaz Mohsin, Adjunct Faculty at Independent University of Bangladesh, North South University, European University of Bangladesh and Canadian University of Bangladesh
