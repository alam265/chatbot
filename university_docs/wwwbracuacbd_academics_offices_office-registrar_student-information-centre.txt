Our Service
The SIC offers a wide range of services, including:
Academic Records:
Assistance with transcripts, certificates, and other academic documents.
Registration Support:
Guidance on course and semester registration, withdrawal, add/drop, retake, repeat, deadlines, and related processes.
Admissions:
Handling admission-related queries.
Student ID Management:
Processing the issuance and reissuance of student ID cards.
Student Account Services:
Support with student accounts and payment-related services.
Information Technology Support:
Assistance with technical issues and services.
Scholarship Information:
Details about available scholarships and application processes.
General Information:
Guidance on academic policies, campus facilities, and procedures.
Liaison with Offices:
Connecting students to relevant academic departments, Student Life, and other support offices for specific concerns.
