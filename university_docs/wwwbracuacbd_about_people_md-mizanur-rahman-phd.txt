Dr. Md. Mizanur Rahman
is an Associate Professor at BRAC Business School, BRAC University, Bangladesh. In 2019, Rahman completed his Ph.D. from Universiti Putra Malaysia (UPM). During his Ph.D., he received the
“Academic Excellence Award”
for 2018/2019 from FEP, UPM. Before that, he completed a BBA with a “
First Position”
and an MBA with a “
Second Position”
from the Department of Management Studies at Rajshahi University, Bangladesh. The areas of research interest for Rahman are management, organizational behavior, HRM, entrepreneurship, artificial intelligence, corporate governance, and sustainability. He has academic research papers in renowned journals such as
Technological Forecasting and Social Change, Journal of Community Psychology, Journal of Enterprising Communities, Journal of Public Affairs, International Journal of Business Excellence, Pertanika Journal of Social Science, and other renowned institutional journals.
He also has book chapters by famous publishers, including
Springer Nature, Elsevier, Taylor & Francis, UPM Press, and World Scientific.
Besides, he has participated in various international and national conferences and has won seven-times the best paper award. Dr. Rahman focuses more on quantitative techniques following PLS-SEM, CB-SEM, and SPSS.
