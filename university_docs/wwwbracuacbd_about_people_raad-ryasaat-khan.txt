Raad Ryasaat Khan
BRAC Business School
raad.khan@bracu.ac.bd
Kha-224 Merul Badda
Dhaka 1212. Bangladesh
is a Lecturer at BRAC Business School, specializing in Management Information Systems (MIS). He brings a diverse international background in business, technology, and data-driven decision-making, with extensive professional experience in the United States across multiple industries.
Since the age of 15, Raad has been an ambassador of Bangladesh as a KL-YES Scholar, a prestigious exchange program funded by the U.S. Department of State. Upon completing the program, he remained actively involved with YES Alumni Bangladesh, leading multiple community service projects, workshops, and training sessions for new scholars.
He holds a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration from Messiah University, Pennsylvania, and a Master’s degree in Information Systems from Baruch College, New York. His professional career spans various sectors, including insurance, finance, and healthcare technology, where he worked as a Product Analyst for a U.S.-based healthcare company, focusing on electronic health records (EHR) systems and digital transformation.
Beyond academia and industry, Raad is a musician, songwriter, and producer. He is the vocalist and guitarist for Ignius (USA) and Vintage Theory (Bangladesh). His experience in leading a band, releasing an album, marketing, financial management, and client relations translates directly into his teaching philosophy—demonstrating how diverse skill sets contribute to business success.
Raad is passionate about the intersection of data, technology, and business and strives to inspire his students to think critically about how these fields shape modern enterprises.
