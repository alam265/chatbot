Dr. Syed Far Abid Hossain is currently working as an Associate Professor at BRAC Business School, BRAC University. He has obtained PhD in Management Science from the School of Management (AACSB Accredited), Xi’an Jiaotong University, PRC. He holds an MBA (Management) degree from the University of Sunderland, UK and a BBA (Marketing) degree from Dhaka University. During his PhD, he received the best publication award by the provincial government. In addition, he was awarded the best CSC scholar award from the Chinese Government in 2018. He was the top CGPA scorer in his PhD batch. Before joining BRAC University, he served as an Asst. Professor and Coordinator, MBA Program at IUBAT – International University of Business Agriculture and Technology where he received the Miyan Publication Award and selected one of the top researchers in 2021 for his outstanding research and publications. Also, Dr. Abid received the best presenter award in the international seminar on emerging challenges and opportunities in business and economy in 2021. Dr. Abid was one of the key organizing committee members for 16th South Asian Management Forum and served as an editorial team member. He also has professional work experience in different national & multinational organizations such as ACI, Robi Axiata Ltd. etc.
Dr. Abid authored 43 academic journal articles, conference papers and book chapters in internationally recognized, peer reviewed and well-indexed Journals (ISI, Scopus, SSCI, SCIE, ABDC, ESCI, EI) from Elsevier, Springer, SAGE, Taylor and Francis, Frontiers, Science Direct, Emerald, MDPI and IGI global. Some of the renowned journals are: Interactive Learning Environments (Q1), Plos One (Q1), Technology, Pedagogy and Education (Q1), International Journal of Gender and Entrepreneurship (Q1), International Journal of Environmental Research and Public Health (Q1), PeerJ (Q1), F1000 Research (Q1), Frontiers in Psychiatry (Q1), Heliyon (Q1), Asia Pacific Journal of Business administration (Q2), SAGE Open (Q2), Journal of Public Health Research (Q2), The Chinese Economy (Q2), Cogent Education (Q2) and so on. Till date, Dr. Abid has 1050+ citations in Google scholar.
Dr. Abid has published numerous book chapters and conference proceedings with famous publishers like Springer Nature and IGI global. His research interests include AI, market turbulence, academic performance, education psychology, education management, technology in education, women entrepreneurship, innovation and media entrepreneurship. Apart from his current roles at BRAC Business School, Dr. Abid served as a reviewer of 33 famous journals from reputed publishers including Elsevier, Wiley, Springer, SAGE, Taylor and Francis, Frontiers, Emerald, IGI Global and MDPI. Dr. Abid severed as an editorial board member of 16th South Asian Management Forum in 2021. Dr. Abid also served as a session chair in various conferences. He is strongly involved with GEPN (Global Education Policy network) for international research collaboration. Till date, Dr. Abid is proudly co-authored with renowned professors and researchers from University of Oxford, UK and many other recognized higher educational institutions from different parts of the world.
