Rubel Saha joined BRAC Business School as a Senior Lecturer in Accounting. Previously he worked at North South University as an Adjunct Lecturer for more than two years. He commenced his academic career at Primeasia University as a full-time lecturer and worked there for nearly five years. He pursued a bachelor degree in Accounting & Finance from North South University with a distinction of Cum Laude. He completed a Master of Commerce degree with a major in Accounting from Curtin University, Australia. He received the
CUPSA Academic Performance Grant at Curtin University.
Rubel's research interest extends to the areas of sustainability accounting and reporting, corporate governance, ESG disclosures, and corporate social responsibility. He published journal articles in internationally reputed Scopus-indexed journals. His research papers have been published in
Accounting Research Journal and Journal of Risk and Financial Management.
