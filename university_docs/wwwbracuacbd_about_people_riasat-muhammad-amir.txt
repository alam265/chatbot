is a PhD candidate in the Department of Strategic Communication, Advertising, and Public Relations at the Autonomous University of Barcelona (UAB), Spain. His research focuses on media communication, with particular interest in crisis communication, public relations, and social media dynamics.
Prior to joining UAB, he spent six years as a faculty member at North South University in Bangladesh. His academic journey began in the United States, where he earned both his bachelor’s and master’s degrees from St. Cloud State University, Minnesota. During his time at St. Cloud State, he also gained practical experience as a Market Analyst Intern at e-2-D Services LLC, Bloomington, MN.
Mr. Amir has been recognized for his academic excellence and leadership abilities. He received the Dean's Award for outstanding performance as a pre-business major in both Fall 2009 and Spring 2010. He was also awarded a leadership and service scholarship by the National Society of Collegiate Scholars and was an active participant in a leadership training program organized by The National Society of Leadership and Success at St. Cloud State University. Furthermore, he was nominated for the “Excellence in Leadership Award” in 2011 and again in 2013.
His core areas of expertise include data analysis, crisis communication, social media communication, and public relations
