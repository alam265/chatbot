Ms. Afsana Akhtar,
now working as an Assistant Professor in BRAC Business School, joined BRAC University as a lecturer in 2005. Since then, apart from teaching she has been actively engaged in academic research and involved with co- curricular activities. Prior to joining BRAC University, she served East West University as Lecturer. She published a good number of research articles in different national and international journals. She received her MBA from of IBA, University of Dhaka. She has been actively involved with student activity and was the founder adviser of BRAC university women sports club, Perecon. She also worked as student counselor and served as Coordinator of the BBA program of BBS.
